About Me

I'm a developer and budding AI researcher from Kashmir with a strong curiosity for how intelligence — both human and artificial — works. My academic journey includes a Master's in Computer Science, which slowly pulled me toward machine learning, deep learning, and eventually explainable AI.

I enjoy studying fairness, bias, and transparency in ML systems. My goal: build AI that is smart, understandable, and fair.

Beyond programming, I’m deeply interested in natural languages, creative thinking, psychology of learning, and how humans interpret intelligence.

Outside the tech world, I love cooking, experimenting with flavors, listening to calming pop tracks, and reflecting on ideas (sometimes a bit too deeply).

I'm someone whose mood shifts, who questions everything around him, and who likes exploring things from every angle — that curiosity fuels my work as well as my personal growth.
